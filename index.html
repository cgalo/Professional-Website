More About Me

I am looking to apply programming, mathematical and technical skills as an intern in the field of computer science.

Hola!

I started my undergraduate at North Park University, where I was a student-athlete majoring in Physics & Engineering, back in 2015. I was part of the cross country team, as well as the track & field team. It was at North Park where I fell in love with coding and econmics and decided to transfer to the University of Toledo to pursue my passion for computer science. I started at UToledo back in Spring 2017 and my expected graduation date is December 2020.

Since then I have been able to be part of different groups that have helped me develop as a person and as professional. Right now I am part of the business fraternity, Pi Sigma Epsilon, where I've had the honor to submerge myself with business skills, create a bigger network and work on projects to keep improving the fraternity and its members. Then there is ACM, Association for Computer Machinery, where I've learned many technical skills. Some of these skills include web development, working as a coding team through git and agile.

Presently I'm an intern at a Associated Spring Raymond as an Information Technology Intern, located in Maumee OH. Since January I've worked with troubleshooting hardware, software and networking issues. I've created powershell and batch scripts to automize imaging process. Also created management tracking system for deployed assets' information, CISCO networking hardware, and static IPs on the network.

I am currently on the hunt for new opportunities to keep learning and expanding my thurst for knowledge, with the goal to become a software developer or a cybersecurity professional after graduation.
